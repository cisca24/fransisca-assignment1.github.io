Introduction:

My name is Francisca Wawira. I am passionate about geospatial technologies and their applications in solving real-world problems. My motivation stems from a fascination with maps and the ability to visualize and analyze spatial data to derive meaningful insights. I believe that geomatics can revolutionize decision-making processes in various fields.

Geomatic Skills:

I have acquired skills in GIS software such as ArcGIS and QGIS, as well as proficiency in data collection techniques using GPS and remote sensing tools like satellite imagery interpretation.

Hobbies:

Outside of academia, I enjoy hiking, photography, and exploring new places. These activities not only refresh my mind but also deepen my appreciation for geographic landscapes and their significance.
